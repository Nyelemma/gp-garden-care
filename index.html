About GP Garden Care

Your local, qualified gardener

GP Garden Care is an affordable, insured and eco-conscious gardening service based in Morecambe, covering Lancaster, Morecambe and the surrounding areas. Established in 2021, we hold a Diploma in Garden Design & Maintenance and were proud winners of the Quality Business Awards in both 2024 and 2025.

Whether you need a regular tidy, a one-off clearance or a complete garden redesign, you’ll get honest advice, a tidy finish and a friendly, reliable service every time.

  • Affordable, transparent pricing
  • Fully insured for your peace of mind
  • Eco-conscious, responsible methods
  • Free, no-obligation quotes
